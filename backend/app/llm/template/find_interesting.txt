## How to decide what to follow up on

Scan the first-pass results and look for exactly one of these four patterns:

**1. A number that raises a follow-up question**
   e.g. correlation salary↔ai_risk = −0.24 → "Does this hold within each country, or is it driven by one outlier country?"
   e.g. top 3 job titles hold 40% of records → "Are these 3 titles also dominating job_openings, or just record count?"

**2. A gap worth quantifying precisely**
   e.g. survival_class=2 has higher average salary - but what is the actual p25/p75 range? Is it a clean separation or heavy overlap?

**4. A claim worth stress-testing across a subgroup**
   Pass 1 found "high salary → lower risk" globally → does this hold when you look only at:

   - above-median job_openings roles only?
   - a specific education level?
